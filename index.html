Hi, I'm Yulin Hao(郝宇琳)!

I am a research master student in Economics at Sciences Po Paris.

My research interests lie in the field of quantitative marketing. Broadly speaking, I intend to employ economic theory, econometrics tools to explore how consumers make decisions, what are the role of heterogeneity and limited rationality in shaping consumer demand, and how to quantify the willingness to pay for unmarketable attributes using stated or revealed preference methods.

Besides research, I love coffee, beer, cigarettes and, my girlfriend. She is a perfect woman. Marrying her is my next research agenda.
