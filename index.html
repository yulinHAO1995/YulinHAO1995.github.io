Hi, I'm Yulin Hao(郝宇琳)!

I am a research master student in Economics at Sciences Po. Before coming to Paris, I studied in Chengdu and Kaohsiung.

My research interests lie in the field of quantitative marketing. Broadly speaking, I intend to employ economic theory, econometric tools to investigate how consumers make decisions, what are the role of heterogeneity and limited rationality in shaping consumers behavior.
